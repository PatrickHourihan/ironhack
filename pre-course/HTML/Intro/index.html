Show.art is a platform for artists to connect with owners and managers of galleries, showrooms, museums, commercial businesses, private residences, and other venues to show their art without having to...

Here's how you can connect with a venue to show your art.

by Patrick Hourihan, not an artist

1. Register

2. Search venues based on location, availability, and daily show rate.

3. Contact a venue representative and submit your portfolio for consideration to ensure the venue is able to maintain a sense of curation if they desire (perhaps a tier system could be developed, a venue like a restaurant or bar may be willing to take any artwork, while a showroom or gallery may be more stringent - for some we could offer direct pay with scheduling)
